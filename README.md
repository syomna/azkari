# أذكاري | Azkari

**أذكاري** هو رفيقك اليومي في طريقك نحو القرب من الله. يقدم لك التطبيق مجموعة متكاملة من الأذكار، القرآن الكريم، ومواقيت الصلاة لترتقي بعبادتك وتقوي علاقتك بالله.

**Azkari** is your daily companion on your path towards closeness to Allah. The app offers a comprehensive suite of tools—including Azkar, the Holy Quran, and Prayer Times—to elevate your worship and strengthen your spiritual journey.

---

### ✨ Features | المميزات

* **📖 Holy Quran & Audio**: Read the Quran or listen to beautiful recitations.
* **🕌 Prayer Times & Azan**: Stay on top of your prayers with accurate timings and Adhan notifications.
* **🧭 Qibla Finder**: Find the direction of the Kaaba from anywhere in the world.
* **📿 Diverse Azkar**: A rich collection of daily supplications (Morning, Evening, Sleep, and more).
* **🔔 Smart Notifications**: Customizable daily reminders for Azkar and prayer alerts.
* **🌐 Offline Access**: Access core Azkar and Quran text even without an internet connection.
